# Finance Dashboard
Created three specialized finance dashboards focused on Sales, Profit, and Unit Sales, along with an index page to navigate among them, is an excellent approach for comprehensive financial monitoring and analysis. Below, I provide an outline and description of what each dashboard might contain and how an index page could be structured to efficiently navigate between them.
# 1. Index Page
Purpose: The index page serves as the central hub from which users can navigate to the specific finance dashboard they need to review. It should be user-friendly and designed to allow quick access to each specialized dashboard.

## Design:

1. Navigation Links: Clear links or buttons labeled "Sales Dashboard," "Profit Dashboard," and "Unit Sales Dashboard."
2. Brief Description: A short description under each link explaining what users can expect to find in each dashboard.
3. Accessibility Features: Ensure that the navigation is accessible, with adequate spacing and responsive design for different devices.

# 2. Sales Dashboard
Purpose: To provide a detailed analysis of overall sales performance, trends, and metrics that impact revenue generation.

# Key Metrics:
1. Total Sales
2. Total Profit
3. Total Unit Sold
4. Total COGS
5. Total Gross Sales
6. Total Sales by Discount Band
7. Total Sales by Product
8. Total Sales By Segment
9. Total Sales By Month Name
    
# 3. Profit Dashboard
Purpose: Focuses on profitability metrics and cost analysis to help understand the financial health of the business beyond just sales numbers.

# Key Metrics:

1. Total Sales
2. Total Profit
3. Total Unit Sold
4. Total COGS
5. Total Gross Sales
6. Total Profit by Discount Band
7. Total Profit by Product
8. Total Profit By Segment
9. Total Profit By Month Name
    
# 4. Unit Sales Dashboard
Purpose: Provides insights into the quantities of units sold, inventory levels, and operational metrics related to the physical volume of goods or services sold.

# Key Metrics:
1. Total Sales
2. Total Profit
3. Total Unit Sold
4. Total COGS
5. Total Gross Sales
6. Total Unit Sold by Discount Band
7. Total Unit Sold by Product
8. Total Unit Sold By Segment
9. Total Unit Sold By Month Name


# PROCESS
1. Data Preparation: Clean and preprocess the dataset, handling missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies.
2. Dashboard Creation: Develop an interactive dashboard using Power BI to track and analyze online sales data. Include widgets and filters to enable user-driven exploration.
3. KPI Visualization: Display total revenue and average sales value prominently on the dashboard using appropriate visualizations such as line charts or KPI cards.
